Delhi Technical Campus

Delhi Technical Campus, located in Greater Noida, is a prominent educational institution offering a range of technical programs in the fields of engineering, architecture, and management.

Established in 1998, the campus is affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, known for its high-quality education and vibrant learning environment. Delhi Technical Campus is renowned for its comprehensive approach to education, ensuring a holistic and constructive learning experience for students.

About Us

Offering dedicated faculty and mentorship to support students' academic and professional development.

Focus on academic excellence and practical exposure, Delhi Technical Campus has a track record of producing successful graduates. The institute's faculty comprises dedicated educators who provide guidance and mentorship to students.

Delhi Technical Campus is known for its strong industry connections, leading to impressive placement opportunities for its students. The campus has a history of placements in renowned companies like IBM, TCS, Cognizant, Byjus, Toppr, and more. Students at Delhi Technical Campus have excelled in various domains, securing placements with attractive packages and gaining valuable experience in their chosen fields.

Business Administration & Commerce

Delhi Technical Campus (DTC) offers a diverse range of programs including Master of Business Administration (MBA), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), and Bachelor of Commerce (BCom). These programs provide students with essential knowledge and skills in business and commerce, preparing them for successful careers in their respective fields.
